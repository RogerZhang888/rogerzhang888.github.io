My Milestone ceremony.
I remember showing up in Clementi Camp on 13 March 2023, having no idea what I was getting into. Subsequently, I was posted to G3 Branch, had a chat with the Department Head, and gradually integrated myself into this new environment. And just like that, 22 months have passed.
First Branch Retreat at Changi Civil Service Club.
The Army is an interesting place. There are uncountable experiences I have had that, safe to say, I will never have in any other setting. It has challenged me, and it has shaped me. I feel fortunate to have met and worked with plenty of genuine, honest people.
Airsoft at HomeTeamNS Khatib!
I look back to my first Branch Cohesion/Retreat, then all the way to my last one. It is a whole “change of guard”, yet the determination and tenacity to strive higher remains. I have nothing but respect and admiration for each and every one who selflessly serves for the defence of our nation.
My last cohesion: Bowling at HomeTeamNS CCK.
Whichever stage of life you are in, give it your best shot and leave with no regrets. I hope I have been a valuable contributor to the Division. It has been a memorable two years. Now it is time to close this door and begin a new chapter in my life. Thanks for everything. SWS
